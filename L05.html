1. Introduction

for (let i = 1; i <= 10; i++) {}

for loop (starts at i (1); i stops at 10; add 1 to i each time)

Much more examples in L05.js

So basically, for loop in the 1st phase we make a variable JUST for the loop, usually its "i"; middle part is a boolean expression, and will continue to execute as long as its true (i (1) IS less than 10, therefore its true); the last part is updating the value of i
